Aditi Kathait
I believe design should feel warm, easy, and inclusive — ensuring no one feels left out, no matter their background or abilities. My journey began in engineering, but my love for art, interior décor, and painting naturally drew me toward UI/UX, where creativity meets empathy and function meets form.

For me, design is a quiet form of healing — a way to bring beauty, positivity, and connection into everyday experiences. Coming from [your place], I carry cultural richness and a deep belief that functionality and accessibility make design truly meaningful.
